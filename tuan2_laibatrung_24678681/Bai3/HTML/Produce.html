Produce

Welcome to our Farm Shop! At Sunny Acres, we take pride in growing the freshest, highest-quality fruits and vegetables in Council Bluffs. All of our produce is grown right here on our farm using sustainable farming practices.

Our Farm Shop is open year-round, offering seasonal produce at its peak of freshness. We believe that farm-fresh produce not only tastes better but is better for you and your family.

Pick Your Own: For the freshest experience possible, we invite you to pick your own fruits and vegetables! There's nothing quite like harvesting your own food straight from the field. It's a fun family activity and a great way to teach children about where their food comes from.

During the growing season, you can pick your own strawberries, raspberries, blueberries, tomatoes, peppers, beans, and more. Call ahead to find out what's currently available for picking.
