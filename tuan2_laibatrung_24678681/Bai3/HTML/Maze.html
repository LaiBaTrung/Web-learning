The Corn Maze

Welcome to the Sunny Acres Corn Maze. We first heard about mazes from agricultural exchange students who came from England in 1985. In Europe, mazes are cut in corn fields after the fields have grown tall and the ears have started to develop. After hearing about the mazes in Europe, we thought it would be a great idea to build one of our own right outside Council Bluffs!

Since 1997, thousands of people have enjoyed our Corn Mazes. We hope that you will be a part of this year's Corn Maze as we did building it! Our family would like to invite your family to come out and visit us at the maze this Fall and Autumn Fest.

Our new 15-acre maze (with over 4 miles of trails to explore!) is filled with twists and turns. While it may seem simple at first glance, getting through it is quite a challenge. As you wind through the maze while taking an interactive quiz, Answer the questions correctly and receive additional clues to solve the maze. For families with young children, our maze is filled with jokes, riddles and quests. Try to be the first in your group to find out your quest card by visiting the secret spots. The maze is the perfect size and height for little ones - standing places and educational displays are scattered throughout.

There is no water available in the maze. Make sure you bring water bottles with you! No one under the age of 12 will be allowed in the maze without an adult at any time.
