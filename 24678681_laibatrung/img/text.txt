About Volunteering

Environmental volunteer organizations are actively involved in projects such as tree planting, litter cleanup, recycling, and community education. 
Notable examples include the Striped Project (recycling), the OU Green Plus Club (tree planting), and youth litter cleanup groups. 
These activities often focus on raising awareness, collecting waste, protecting natural resources, and organizing "trad your trash for a gift" events.
